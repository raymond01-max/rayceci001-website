Our Job info/services

At poultrytech Nigeria we inform and guide the existing poultry farmers towards attaining a great potential and maximizing profit in poultry farm enterprise. We give reliable information on weather condition suitable for the birds by using suitable tools and mechanism. We also provide/inform our clients on proper medication/vaccination suitable for their birds which is done periodically. we encourage and teach our clients on the need for proper accounting records by making use of mordern technology/data analyses to ensure accurate records. Now the most important reason for the establishment of poultrytech Nigeria is to bring together proffessionals in the field like the Vetinary doctors, Animal scientist/ experience poultry consultants with our clients( poultry farmers) notwithstanding the capacity of the individual farmer (either large or small scale farm), for inspecting and thorough supervision of our clients poultry farm in affordable and efficient means. We also offer a seminar/workshop for young intending farmers who wish to grab a space on poultry business, we help groom them in other to attain maximum result and efficiency.
